A Short Trip from Chennai (Tamil Nadu) to Varkala (Kerala)

  • Atharsh, Arjun, Abdul, Ganesh, Kiranraj
  • Travel

Varkala is a coastal town located in the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, featuring a long, pristine beach along the Arabian Sea and a unique geological formation of red cliffs that overlook the shoreline. Here are some key features and attractions of Varkala:

Varkala Beach: Varkala's primary attraction is its beautiful beach, which stretches along the coastline. The beach is relatively less crowded compared to some of Kerala's other popular beaches, making it a peaceful and serene destination. Visitors can relax on the golden sands, swim in the sea, and enjoy the picturesque sunsets.

Red Cliffs: The red cliffs that line the beach area are a distinctive feature of Varkala. They not only add to the area's natural beauty but also provide excellent vantage points for panoramic views of the sea. Many restaurants and guesthouses are built into these cliffs, offering stunning ocean vistas.

Local Cuisine: Kerala is famous for its delicious cuisine, and Varkala is no exception. You can savor authentic Keralan dishes, including fresh seafood, at the local restaurants and street food stalls.

" Kerala is a place where the GOD's live."

Varkala's unique combination of natural beauty, spirituality, and recreational activities makes it a popular destination for travelers seeking a laid-back beach experience in Kerala. It's worth noting that the town can get quite crowded during the peak tourist season, so if you prefer a quieter experience, consider visiting during the off-season.
